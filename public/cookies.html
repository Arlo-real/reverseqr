Why There's No Cookie Banner

We Don't Use Cookies

ReverseQR doesn't use cookies at all. No tracking cookies, no analytics cookies, no session cookies—nothing. That's why you don't see a cookie consent banner.

How Sessions Work Without Cookies

Instead of cookies, ReverseQR uses temporary session codes. When you create a session, a unique code is generated that exists only in the URL and in memory. This code is never stored in your browser's cookie storage.

Truly Temporary Sessions

Sessions are completely ephemeral. They exist only for the duration of your file transfer and are automatically deleted after a short period of inactivity. When you close the page or refresh, the connection is gone—there's nothing stored that could identify you later.

No Local Storage Either

We don't use localStorage, sessionStorage, or IndexedDB to persist any data. Your encryption keys are generated fresh for each session and exist only in memory while the page is open.

Privacy by Design

This cookie-free approach is part of our commitment to privacy. By not using cookies or any persistent client-side storage, we ensure that there's no trace of your activity left on your device after you're done using ReverseQR. (except in browser history)
