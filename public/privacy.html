Privacy Policy

ReverseQR

Our Commitment to Privacy

ReverseQR is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect minimal data and use end-to-end encryption to protect your communications.

End-to-End Encryption

All messages and files are encrypted in your browser using AES-256-GCM before transmission. The server never has access to your encryption keys or plaintext content.

Data We Collect

Session Data: Temporary session codes and encrypted messages stored for a maximum of 15 minutes.
Files: Encrypted files are stored temporarily and automatically deleted after 30 minutes.
Log Data: IP addresses and timestamps for security and abuse prevention (no message content).

Data We Don't Collect

• No user accounts or personal information (since we don't ask for it)
• No cookies or tracking
• No access to decrypted content
• No permanent storage of messages or files

Data Retention

All session data and files are automatically deleted after their retention period expires. We do not keep any long-term records of transferred content (this would be useless since we can't decrypt them).

Third Parties

We do not share any data with third parties. There are no analytics, advertising, or tracking services integrated into this application.
